Congratulations on being approved for the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)! This federal program is a fantastic step toward making oral healthcare more accessible. However, many patients feel confused about what to do next after receiving their welcome letter from Sun Life.

If you are looking for a CDCP provider in Surrey, BC, Canary Dental is here to help. Here is your step-by-step guide on how to use your new benefits.

## Step 1: Wait for Your Start Date

Your welcome package from Sun Life will clearly state your **coverage start date**. It is crucial that you do not book a dental appointment before this date, as the CDCP will not retroactively pay for services rendered prior to your active coverage period.

## Step 2: Find a Participating CDCP Dentist

Not every dental clinic in Canada participates in the CDCP program. When calling a clinic, your first question should be: *"Are you currently accepting new patients under the CDCP?"*

At [Canary Dental in Surrey](/), we happily welcome CDCP patients. We believe that everyone deserves high-quality, compassionate dental care.

## Step 3: Bring the Right Documents to Your Appointment

When you arrive for your first visit, our friendly reception team will need to create your patient profile and set up your insurance billing. Please bring:
1.  Your physical or digital **Sun Life CDCP Member Card**.
2.  A valid piece of **government-issued ID**.

*We also have Mandarin-speaking staff available if you require assistance in Chinese ([中文牙醫服務](/mandarin-speaking-dentist)).*

## Step 4: The "Pre-Determination" Process

Before any dental work begins—whether it's a [routine cleaning](/dental-cleaning) or a [tooth extraction](/wisdom-tooth-extraction)—our team will perform what is called a "pre-determination."

We submit a proposed treatment plan directly to Sun Life through a secure portal. Within minutes, we will receive a response detailing exactly what the CDCP will cover and what your out-of-pocket "co-payment" (if any) will be. This means **no surprise bills**.

## Step 5: Direct Billing

You do not need to worry about paying the full amount upfront and waiting weeks for a reimbursement check. As a participating provider, Canary Dental submits the claim directly to Sun Life on your behalf. You are only responsible for paying your co-payment portion at the end of your visit.

Don't let your benefits go unused! Preventative care is the best way to avoid painful dental emergencies. Check out our [CDCP info page](/cdcp-dentist) or use our [LINE booking system](https://line.me/ti/p/4RpjC2r6Hq) to schedule your first appointment.
