## What is the CDCP?

The [Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)](https://www.canada.ca/en/services/benefits/dental/canadian-dental-care-plan.html) is a federal program designed to reduce financial barriers to oral healthcare for uninsured Canadians with an adjusted family net income of less than $90,000.

## What Services Are Covered in 2026?

The CDCP focuses primarily on **preventative and restorative dentistry**. While it doesn’t cover cosmetic procedures like teeth whitening or porcelain veneers, it does cover many essential services necessary to maintain a healthy mouth. 

Typically, the CDCP may cover a portion or all of the following services (subject to specific frequency limitations and pre-authorization):

*   **Preventative Services:** Routine examinations (check-ups), professional dental cleanings (scaling and polishing), and preventative fluoride treatments.
*   **Diagnostic Services:** Digital X-rays required to detect cavities and monitor bone health.
*   **Restorative Services:** Basic fillings to treat cavities and repair minor tooth decay.
*   **Oral Surgery:** Standard tooth extractions (including basic wisdom tooth removal).
*   **Endodontics:** Root canal treatments for infected teeth (often requires pre-authorization).
*   **Periodontics:** Deep scaling and root planing for gum disease.

*Note: Complex treatments like crowns or partial dentures have strict criteria and almost always require pre-authorization from Sun Life.*

## Understanding Co-Payments

It is a common misconception that the CDCP is "100% free" for everyone. The CDCP operates on a **co-payment structure** based on your adjusted family net income:

1.  **Under $70,000:** The CDCP covers 100% of the *eligible CDCP fees*. (Note: Clinic fees may differ slightly from the CDCP fee guide, resulting in a small balance).
2.  **$70,000 to $79,999:** The CDCP covers 60% of eligible fees. The patient is responsible for a 40% co-payment.
3.  **$80,000 to $89,999:** The CDCP covers 40% of eligible fees. The patient is responsible for a 60% co-payment.
